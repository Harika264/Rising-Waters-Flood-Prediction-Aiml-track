METEOROLOGICAL WARNING: HIGH FLOOD RISK

Extreme precipitation thresholds exceeded. Immediate preparedness is advised.

Immediate Action Items
  • Identify high ground or designated local flood shelters.
  • Secure critical personal documents and create an emergency "go-bag".
  • Elevate valuable electronics, furniture, and appliances off the floor.
  • Clear drains, gutters, and downspouts around residential properties.
Safety Protocols
  • Avoid walking, swimming, or driving through moving water. Just 6 inches of moving water can knock you down.
  • Monitor local weather broadcasts and emergency services SMS alerts.
  • Keep contact numbers for emergency response services handy.
  • If advised to evacuate, do so immediately without delay.
